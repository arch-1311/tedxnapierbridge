Your TED Experience

The TED Conference experience is like none other. Whether you’re a TED enthusiast or it’s your very first time joining us, this dynamic week is sure to become a highlight of your year.

From the electric moments in the TED Theater, when a talk resonates so deeply that you are overtaken by goosebumps, to the joy of making an unexpected new connection that lasts a lifetime.

It's an enduring rush of inspiration. Get a glimpse of what we’re planning for you at TED2025.

Your community

TED brings together a unique group of thinkers, doers, leaders and dreamers — a wide spectrum of individuals whose humility, kindness and generosity speak louder than their brilliance.

Members of the TED community are on a journey of lifelong learning, with fierce determination to make the world a better place.

Get ready, because at TED there are no strangers, just friends you’ve not yet met!

The TED theater

Architect and experience designer David Rockwell crafted the incredible pop-up theater for TED, using 8,000 custom pieces of Douglas Fir. Each year, a dedicated team lovingly assembles this architectural wonder, and after five days, the air is filled with a fresh, woody aroma, signaling that “it’s time for TED.”

The theater’s closeness to the speakers creates a communal vibe similar to that of a campfire. Every spot offers the ultimate viewing experience with a mix of seating options like couches, balcony spots, cozy chairs and wheelchair-accessible seating.

Discovery Sessions

Not all great ideas are shared from a stage; some require closer exploration. Discovery Sessions bring ideas to life through expert-led experiences, in-the-field workshops, activities and excursions, all curated with an unparalleled understanding of what makes these sessions sing (yes, sometimes literally).

Sign up for bucket list activities that get you out, moving and interacting with ideas and connecting with others in unexpected ways.

Breaks for Connection

A vital part of the TED experience, networking breaks are dedicated times for you to build deep human connections — one-on-one or in groups.

Immerse yourself in dynamic discussions and serendipitous encounters with attendees and speakers over a barista-brewed coffee or snack.
