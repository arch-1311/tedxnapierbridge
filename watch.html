Talks

TEDxNapierBridge has been dedicated to spreading innovative and thought-provoking ideas through short, powerful talks since its inception in 2015.TEDxNapierBridge talks have covered a wide range of topics, from technology and entrepreneurship to art and social impact. The event has featured a diverse range of speakers, including entrepreneurs, artists, activists, and thought leaders, who have shared their unique perspectives and experiences.

These talks have not only inspired thousands of people in Chennai but have also played a significant role in shaping local conversations and driving positive change in the community. Whether you're looking to explore new ideas, challenge your perspectives, or simply be inspired, TEDxNapierBridge talks have something to offer everyone.
